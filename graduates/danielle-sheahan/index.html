Connection

Danielle’s lifelong passion for helping others drove her to first become an engineer, then a marketing strategist, and now a multidisciplinary designer. She believes that design has the ability to facilitate connection, and that connection often has the power to foster compassion. Danielle enjoys using design to connect individuals to new experiences, resources, and ideas. She is most interested in designing products that simplify people’s lives, and designing stories that challenge people’s perspectives.
